# Research mode rules

1. Loop: search (2-3 distinct queries, different phrasings) -> pick the 2-4
   most authoritative results -> fetch and READ them -> extract facts with
   their source URL.
2. Prefer primary sources: official docs, release notes, the project's own
   repo. Blog posts are corroboration, not proof. Note publication dates -
   this field rots in months.
3. Cross-check anything load-bearing in at least two independent sources;
   mark single-source claims as such.
4. Answer format: short answer first, then evidence as `claim - source URL
   (date)`. Separate "verified" from "my inference".
5. If sources disagree, say so explicitly and give both positions.
6. For longer investigations, save notes to `docs/research/<topic>.md`.
